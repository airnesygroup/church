Our Pillars

Our Mission and Vision.

Mission : To invite all who are weary, burdened, and lost to find rest, peace, and renewal in Christ. Guided by the words of Matthew 11:28-30, the mission emphasizes teaching others to follow Christ's example of humility, gentleness, and love, offering them the true peace that only He can provide.

Vision : Inspired by Matthew 28:18-20 and John 10:27-28, the vision calls for a world where disciples of Christ are made in all nations. It envisions a movement where God's authority, granted to His followers, leads to the spread of His teachings, baptizing and guiding others in faith. It highlights a time when no human power can undermine the eternal life promised by Christ, and no authority or ideology can overpower His truth and love.

Our Reflection speaks to the struggles faced by past generations and the current Gen Z, critiquing various powers, including political, religious, and social authorities. Yet, amid this turbulence, the message calls for a return to the unchanging voice of Christ, the Prince of Peace, who offers eternal life and security.
